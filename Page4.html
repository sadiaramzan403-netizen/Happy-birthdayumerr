My Prayers for You

May Allah bless you with the best.
May Allah grant you the success that sustains.
May Allah make you a person He loves.
May Allah make you a great son, brother, husband and father.
May Allah ease every pain you come across.
May Allah never ever make you go through any pain or suffering that can stay with you.
May Allah always keep you on a righteous path.
May Allah keep you with me till the day I die.
May Allah always protect you from evil.
May Allah always protect your dignity.
May Allah protect you from bad company and bad influence.
May Allah bless you with more than you deserve.

(Ameen sum Ameen)
